Your One-Stop Website for Maid's Matter

Journey Care Employment Agency is committed in connecting people with different culture and help them to settle in our Singapore environment while providing the best helper for your household needs. We are a fully accredited and licensed employment agency in Singapore, established since 2008. Our agency specializes in sourcing for domestic helpers from Myanmar (formerly known as Burma). We are also able to arrange for maids from India, Indonesia and the Philippines.

Due to the hospitable and simple nature of the Burmese people, the demand for Myanmar maids has risen. They are popular for their reputably good working attitudes and simple personalities. Currently, there are more than 25000 hired in Singapore.

A perfect home deserves a dedicated helper. Not only the helpers from our agency are known to be honest and respectable, they are also obedient and hardworking. Each of our candidates undergo a careful and strict screening process by our various recruiters in Myanmar, to ensure the helper we provide you maintains the highest standard of quality. We will carry out a detailed selection process to consider the best match of our candidates’ skills to clients needs.

Our company guarantees a professional and transparent transaction, with no hidden fees. Our agency takes care of all the necessary arrangements on your behalf, and in most cases your chosen worker will arrive within two weeks upon approval from MOM.

Our services are available to you in customised packages. Part of our services list includes Free Replacement, Counselling, Documentation and etc…
